About

Biography

Armughan Muqeem, a renowned name in the world of sports and business, has achieved remarkable success at a young age. With a passion for bodybuilding and an entrepreneurial spirit, he has made significant contributions to both the sports and business arenas.

About

Accomplishments In the World of Sports

Accomplishments

Youngest Judge at Asian Bodybuilding and Physique Sports Championships 2022

Armughan Muqeem's dedication to the sport of bodybuilding led to his recognition as the youngest judge at the prestigious Asian Bodybuilding and Physique Sports Championships in 2022. His discerning eye and deep knowledge of the sport have made him a valuable asset in the world of bodybuilding.

Scheduled to Officiate at the 13th World Championship

With his growing reputation and expertise, Armughan Muqeem is set to officiate as a judge at the upcoming 13th World Championship, further solidifying his role as a respected authority in international bodybuilding competitions.

Executive Board Member at ABBF (Asian Bodybuilding and Fitness Federation

I am honored to serve as an Executive Board Member at ABBF, where I actively contribute to the growth and development of the organization.

International Judge at ABBF

My role as an International Judge at ABBF allows me to play a crucial part in evaluating and recognizing talent in the world of bodybuilding and fitness.

International Judge at WBPF Championships

As an International Judge at the WBPF Championships, I have the privilege of officiating prestigious international competitions.

In the Business World

Successful Businessman and Entrepreneur

Beyond his accomplishments in the realm of sports, Armughan Muqeem has carved out a successful career in business. He is the CEO of All Precision Equipment Co., a company known for its commitment to quality and precision in the field of equipment manufacturing.

Director, Procurement at Al-Prince Engineer

Additionally, Armughan serves as the Director of Procurement at Al-Prince Engineer, where he applies his astute business acumen to manage the procurement functions of the company.

Vice President of the Pakistan Body Building Federation

Armughan Muqeem is also the Vice President of the Pakistan Body Building Federation, demonstrating his dedication to the development and promotion of bodybuilding in his home country.
